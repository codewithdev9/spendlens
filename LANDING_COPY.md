## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes, completely. No credit card, no login. You enter your tools, get your audit, see your savings — all without an account. We only ask for your email if you want to save the report or be notified of future optimizations.

**Q: What AI tools does it cover?**
A: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude (all plans including API), ChatGPT (all plans including API), Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf. That covers 95% of the AI spend we see at early-stage startups.

**Q: How are the savings calculated?**
A: Every number is based on official vendor pricing (sources cited in our PRICING_DATA file, updated monthly). The audit checks: are you on the right plan for your team size, is there a cheaper same-vendor plan that fits, do you have redundant tools, and are you paying retail when discounted credits are available through Credex.

**Q: What does Credex get out of this?**
A: For audits showing over $500/month in savings, we surface Credex as a way to capture additional savings via discounted AI credits. We only mention Credex when it's genuinely relevant to your situation — we don't manufacture savings to push a sale. If you're spending efficiently, we tell you so.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Your tool and spending data is used only to generate your audit. We don't sell it or share it with AI vendors. The public share URL strips your email and company name — only tools and savings numbers are visible publicly.
