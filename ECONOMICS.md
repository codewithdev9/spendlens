# Unit Economics

## What a Converted Lead Is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. Assume:
- Average deal size: $5,000 (company buys 6 months of discounted Anthropic/OpenAI credits upfront)
- Gross margin on credits: 20–35% (Credex sources at 40-60% discount, sells at 20-30% discount)
- Gross profit per deal: $1,000–$1,750
- Customer LTV (2 credit buys/year × 2 years average): $4,000–$7,000 gross profit

For this analysis: **conservative LTV = $4,000 gross profit per converted customer.**

## CAC by Channel

| Channel | Effort | Estimated CAC | Notes |
|---------|--------|--------------|-------|
| Credex sales team upsell | Low | ~$0 | Using existing sales capacity |
| Indie Hackers post | 2 hrs | ~$5 | Time cost only; ~50 users/post |
| HN Show HN | 1 hr | ~$10 | If it lands; 500–2000 users |
| r/ExperiencedDevs | 2 hrs | ~$20 | ~100 users; warm community |
| Cold DM outreach | 4 hrs/20 DMs | ~$50 | 2–3 conversions/20 DMs |
| Content SEO | 10 hrs/post | ~$100 | 6 month lag; long-tail "cursor vs copilot" |

Blended CAC estimate at 30-day volume: **~$30 per email capture** (weighted toward organic)

## Conversion Funnel

```
Visits → Audit completions → Email captures → Consultation booked → Credit purchase

100 visits
  → 35 audit completions (35% — high intent tool, clear CTA)
    → 7 email captures (20% of completions — post-value gate)
      → 1.4 consultations booked (20% of captures — only high-savings cases prompted)
        → 0.7 purchases (50% close rate — strong fit, warm lead)
```

**Revenue per 100 visitors:** 0.7 × $5,000 = $3,500 gross revenue / 0.7 × $1,400 = $980 gross profit

**Effective CPV (cost per visit to profit):** At $30 blended CAC per capture:
- 7 email captures = $210 total CAC
- Gross profit from those 7 = $980
- **LTV:CAC = 4.7x** — healthy for a B2B lead-gen tool

## What It Takes to Drive $1M ARR in 18 Months

$1M ARR = $83,333/month credit sales = ~17 deals/month at $5k avg

Working backwards:
- 17 purchases/month → 34 consultations → 170 email captures → 850 audit completions → 2,400 monthly visits

**At 18 months, SpendLens needs to deliver 2,400 audits/month to Credex's funnel.**

Is this achievable? Let's check:
- Month 1–3: Organic launch, IH + HN: 500–2,000 audits total
- Month 4–6: SEO starts kicking in for "AI tool pricing" keywords: 500/mo
- Month 7–12: Embeddable widget in 5 engineering blogs × 200 readers each = 1,000/mo
- Month 13–18: Referral loop compounds, Credex sales team onboarded to pitch it: 2,500+/mo

**Conclusion:** $1M ARR is achievable in 18 months if:
1. The Credex sales team consistently uses SpendLens in discovery (unfair channel)
2. At least one piece of content goes viral on HN/Twitter in the first 60 days
3. Average deal size stays ≥$3,000 (don't compete on price at the bottom)

The biggest risk: Credex's credit inventory for specific vendors (e.g., Anthropic) may be inconsistent. If supply dries up for a quarter, the consultation-to-purchase conversion drops to near zero.
